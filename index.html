Weight Stairs

What is it?

This is a calorie calculator. Specifically, for one's Basal Metabolic Rate(BMR) and/or Total Daily Energy Expenditure. Not only these, but it also creates a 90 day calories intake plan.

Why should I know about my BMR?

Everyone needs a certain amount of calories just to function. From breathing, digestion, blood flow, brain functionality, etc. Without the minimum calories, a person can't function properly. Everyone's BMR is different; it depends on age, weight, height, and gender.

Why should I know about my TDEE?

It's important to know about BMR. However, a person typically goes about their day doing certain things, especially exercising. So a person is bound to use up more calories. And if a person uses up more calories, a well meaning weight loss plan can backfire. Like BMR, everyone's TDEE is different. Same factors as BMR, but with the addition of activity level.

How does all this help with my fitness goal?(Ex: Weight loss or weight gain)

In order to lose weight, a person needs to go into a calorie deficit, and vice versa. Therefore, in order to get a proper plan, a person needs to know their TDEE. That way a person wouldn't have to take in too many calories, or accidently go into starvation mode, meaning that the metabolism slows down in order to "survive".

Disclaimer: This is not a 100% accurate plan. This is more a general road map for the desired goal. Please consult a doctor or a nutritionist for an accurate plan.
